About PhishGuard

PhishGuard combines a BERT‑based Transformer architecture with low‑level GPU acceleration to identify malicious URLs, email content, and phishing attempts entirely offline. Every analysis stays on your machine — no cloud, no tracking, no latency. We make enterprise‑grade security accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Powerful by design

⚡

GPU accelerated

Native CUDA kernels and TensorRT inference deliver sub‑10ms scanning, even for large email threads. Perfect for real‑time browser protection.

🔒

Zero‑trust privacy

No telemetry, no external API calls. The model runs inside a secure sandbox — your browsing data never leaves the endpoint.

🧠

Offline BERT

Fine‑tuned on millions of phishing samples, our model detects zero‑day attacks and obfuscated malicious content without an internet connection.

How it works

1

Scan

PhishGuard hooks into your browser or email client, extracting URLs and text content.

2

Analyze

On‑device Transformer model assigns a threat score using attention mechanisms.

3

Alert

If malicious intent is detected (>0.95 confidence), you receive a clear warning.
